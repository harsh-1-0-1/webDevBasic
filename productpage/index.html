Overview

The Maruti Suzuki Gypsy is a 4x4 light utility vehicle, known for its rugged frame, excellent off-road performance, and reliability in the harshest terrains. Whether you're crossing rivers, climbing mountains, or just cruising through rugged roads — Gypsy does it all.

  • Engine: 1.3L MPFI Petrol
  • Transmission: 5-speed Manual
  • Drive Type: Selectable 4WD
  • Body Type: Soft Top / Hard Top
